Search

Heatmap shows the frequency of motion on the screen in color. You can search Heatmap data and confirm results from ‘Heatmap Search’.

Note

  • In order to use the Heatmap search function, you need to activate it by selecting [Enable Heatmap] from [Statistics]>[Heatmap]>[Setup].
  • If [Enable Heatmap] is not selected from [Statistics]>[Heatmap]>[Setup], the [Setup] button will appear. If you click the [Setup] button, you will move to the Heatmap setup page.

Heatmap

Heatmap information based on the frequency of motions in the current image will appear on the screen. For the frequency of motion by color to be shown on the screen, refer to the color in [Index].

Index

The Index color bar indicates the frequency of motions. If the frequency of motions is low, it is displayed in blue, and in red if the frequency of motions is high.

Search

When you select [Recent], you can search by recent day, week or month. When you select [Period], you can specify a daily, monthly, or yearly period for searching.

The Heatmap search results will be displayed on the screen immediately.

Searching Heatmap and viewing results

  1. Select the date you wish to search from [Date] in [Search].
  2. Click the [Search] button.
  3. The search results will be displayed in [Results].

Results

If you have searched Heatmap data, the results will be displayed. Heatmap information for the period you have searched, with the image at the time of searching as the background, will be displayed.

Download

When you click the [Download] button after searching, you can download the search results as an image file. The search results can be downloaded in .png format; the downloaded file will be saved in the browser’s default save path. Up to 64 characters, including letters and numbers, can be entered for the file name.
