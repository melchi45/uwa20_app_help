RS-485

Sets the link value between the controller and the camera to enable the control of the camera PTZ through the controller connected to the RS-485 terminal of the camera. When you complete the setting, click the [Apply] button at the bottom of the page.

RS-485 setup

Sets the link value between the controller and PTZ camera.

Protocol

Chooses the communication protocol of the controller and PTZ camera.

Camera ID

Selects camera ID.

Baud rate

Sets the communication speed of RS-485.

Termination

You can set whether or not to terminate the camera in the farthest path in the communication loop between the controller and the camera in order to prevent communication signal attenuation between the controller and PTZ camera.

Note

  • For this operation, the network camera and the controller must be connected normally. The serial port must also be set for the controller to control the camera.
  • A function that is not supported may exist depending on the specification of the controller or the protocol. Refer to the product manual for details.
