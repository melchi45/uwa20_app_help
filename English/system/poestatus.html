PoE output status

PoE extender camera has RJ-45 input and output ports. If an additional network camera is connected to the output port, the power of PoE extender camera uses its own power to power the network camera and enable network communication.

Note

  • If PoE extender camera's output power exceeds 12.95 W, the power of any connected network camera will shut off automatically. To restore the power of network camera,
    • Uncheck the [Enable] box and click [Apply]. Reselect [Enable] and click [Apply].
    • Boot the PoE extender camera to power it on using its own power source.

PoE output status

Controls whether or not to energize the network camera connected to the output port of PoE extender camera, and displays the amount of requested power consumption.

Port

The output port number of PoE extender camera.

Enable

To provide PoE power to the camera of the [Port], click [Enable].

Power consumption

Shows the amount of PoE power consumption.

In [Total PoE power consumption], it shows the total amount of output port power consumption used by network camera connected to PoE extender camera.
